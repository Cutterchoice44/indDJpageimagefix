Privacy Policy for Cutters Choice Radio Alexa Skill

Last updated: 6 September 2025

Cutters Choice Radio (“we”, “us”, “our”) does not collect, store, or sell any personal information through the Cutters Choice Radio Alexa Skill. We do not run user accounts in the skill, and we do not retain any data on our servers from your interactions with the skill.

How the skill works

The skill streams audio from our radio provider via a secure Icecast endpoint operated by Radio Cult and uses Amazon’s AudioPlayer interface to play the stream on your Alexa-enabled device.

Information processed by third parties

Although we don’t collect data via the skill, the following third parties process information needed to operate your device and the audio stream:

  • Amazon (Alexa) may process voice interactions, device identifiers, and usage telemetry under Amazon’s own privacy notices. See the Amazon Alexa Privacy Notice.
  • Radio Cult (streaming) operates the Icecast server that delivers the audio stream. Their infrastructure may log standard technical data (e.g., IP address, timestamps) for security and reliability. We do not receive personally identifying listener analytics from them.

Children’s privacy

The skill is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information via this skill, please contact us and we will investigate and take appropriate action.

Your rights

As a New Zealand-based service, we aim to align with the Privacy Act 2020 (NZ). Depending on your location, you may have rights to access or request deletion of personal information held by third parties (e.g., Amazon). Please consult those providers’ privacy notices for how to exercise your rights with them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be highlighted for clarity.

Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at castingnz@yahoo.co.nz.
